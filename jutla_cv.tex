\section{Ongoing Research/Educational Projects}

\subsection{Exploratory study of psychogenic non-epileptic seizures in adolescents}
\begin{flushleft}Principal Investigator: Sigita Plioplys, MD\\
The study aims to compare brain structure and function between adolescents with psychogenic non-epileptic seizures and controls. My role has been to assist in formulating a hypothesis regarding resting-state functional connectivity and to conduct subsequent data analysis.\end{flushleft}

\subsection{Effectiveness of undergraduate medical education in preparing pediatricians to manage psychiatric illness}
\begin{flushleft}Principal Investigator: James MacKenzie, DO\\
The study aims to survey pediatric residents' exposure to and comfort with managing psychiatric illness. My role has been to assist in developing the survey instrument we used, to facilitate data collection, and to provide mentorship to a medical student working on the project.\end{flushleft}
